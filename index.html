Our Story

Crafted with intention since 2009

Founded by Danish designer Jannik Olander in Los Angeles, Nialaya bridges Eastern mysticism with Western luxury. Every piece is hand-strung in our Hollywood studio, cleansed with sage, and designed to carry meaning beyond the material.

From the sacred temples of Bali to the vibrant streets of Hollywood, our designs carry the energy of cultures that inspire us. Each gemstone is selected for its unique properties — turquoise for protection, howlite for calm, onyx for strength.
